About

The Conference

Focused. Research-driven. Built for academic excellence and global collaboration.

The International Conference on “Recent Advances in Biological Sciences (Biospectrum 2K26)”, hosted by Department of Biosciences, offers a dynamic platform to explore cutting-edge developments across biological sciences. Join us at Mar Athanasios College for Advanced Studies Tiruvalla (Autonomous), for insightful sessions by renowned experts and an opportunity to present your research.

MACFAST

Mar Athanasios College For Advanced Studies Tiruvalla (Autonomous) is a prestigious postgraduate research institute affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam, and approved by AICTE and UGC under section 2(f), New Delhi. The college has been reaccredited with an ‘A+’ Grade by NAAC and is recognized by DSIR. MACFAST is ranked in the 201–300 band by NIRF.

The college offers postgraduate programmes including Biotechnology, Bioinformatics, Food Technology and Quality Assurance, Computer Applications, Social Work, and Management Studies, along with undergraduate and Ph.D. programmes(BioScience and Management Studies).

Associations

Partner institutions

International and national academic associations supporting the conference.

NIOH, Johannesburg, South Africa

The National Institute for Occupational Health, Division of the National Health Laboratory Service, supports public health through disease surveillance, outbreak response, diagnostic services, research and training in health sciences.

KSCSTE-JNTBGRI

Jawaharlal Nehru Tropical Botanical Garden and Research Institute is a premier R&D center dedicated to conservation and sustainable utilization of tropical plant diversity, with research in taxonomy, biotechnology, phytochemistry and ethnomedicine.

Kerala Botanical Society

The Kerala Botanical Society is a professional body dedicated to advancement of botanical sciences and connects traditional plant knowledge with modern scientific research.
